# Search Strings

## Primary Search (Cloud Cost + ML)

### IEEE Xplore
```
("cloud cost" OR "cloud pricing" OR "FinOps" OR "cloud financial")
AND
("machine learning" OR "deep learning" OR "prediction" OR "optimization" OR "LLM")
```
- Fields: Full text
- Date range: 2019-2025
- Document type: Journal articles, Conference papers

### ACM Digital Library
```
[[Abstract: cloud cost] OR [Abstract: finops] OR [Abstract: cloud spend]]
AND
[[Abstract: machine learning] OR [Abstract: deep learning] OR [Abstract: anomaly detection]]
```
- Date range: 2019-2025
- Content type: Research articles, Proceedings

### Springer Link
```
("cloud cost optimization" OR "FinOps")
AND
("machine learning" OR "reinforcement learning" OR "deep learning")
```
- Date range: 2019-2025
- Content type: Articles, Conference papers

### ScienceDirect
```
(cloud AND cost AND (optimization OR prediction))
AND
(machine learning OR deep learning OR AI)
```
- Date range: 2019-2025
- Article type: Research articles, Review articles

### arXiv
```
cat:cs.DC AND (cloud cost OR FinOps) AND (machine learning OR LLM)
```
- Categories: cs.DC, cs.LG, cs.AI, cs.SE
- Date range: 2019-2025
